PORTFOLIO

(De)composing is a genre-blending puzzle platformer where music is intertwined with every mechanic. Take the role of Soviet composer Gregor Ivanovich, an unfulfilled genius who died with his magnum opus incomplete. Guide his restless ghost through each room of the symphony hall, using the peculiar arrangement of musical instruments to communicate his ideas to the student completing his work. Solve each conundrum with your puzzling smarts and platforming rhythm to construct Gregor's symphony one phrase at a time!

What I Did

I was the UI & Menu Designer, UI programmer for the game. I crafted visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces for the game, with a strong emphasis on user experience and aesthetics. I designed and mocked up various menus, including main menus, in-game menus, settings, and HUD elements. Additionally, I developed and implemented interactive UI systems to ensure menus were user-friendly. Through iterative fine-tuning, I optimized UI elements for better accessibility and user satisfaction. Prioritizing user feedback, I made data-driven decisions to continually improve the menus.
