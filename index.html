Floral Fungi

HTML, Css, JavaScript, Node.Js, React

Floral Fungi, is an idle game where you buy and manage a team of sentient mushrooms to collect beautiful flowers for you. Watch your mushroom squad do the work as you earn flowers to purchase even more mushrooms and expand your collection. The game seamlessly stores each session's progress, ensuring your data is securely linked to your login information. Whether you're taking a break or returning for more floral adventures, your journey will be seamlessly preserved. Additionally, periodic auto-saves throughout the session offer peace of mind and convenience. Spring 2023

Media Library

HTML, Css, JavaScript, Node.Js

Media Library is a Node.js project that allows users to log, rate, and track their progress on movies, TV shows, and documentaries. Users can rate videos with a star system, select genres, and add notes. The personalized tracking feature lets users keep track of where they left off in a series. The back-end efficiently stores user data while providing a fast and responsive user experience. The video logging website is a user-friendly platform for tracking, rating, and exploring video media. Mushroom Forest

HTML, Css+Bulma, JavaScript

In "Mushroom Forest," the user explores and interacts with mushrooms in their environment, using drag, freeze, and fling commands. The sun/moon/time orb can be used to change the time of day, while changing the weather affects the mushrooms' behavior, causing them to seek shelter or slow down. A snail can be summoned to scare the mushrooms away, and flower crowns can be spawned for the mushrooms to wear when the user is close by. Fall 2022

Loot Quest

JavaScript,Pixi.js, HTML5, CSS

Loot Quest is an exciting game developed with PIXI.js, challenging users to collect loot while dodging enemies attempting to halt their progress. Collecting all of the loot in each of the 5 stages allows players to progress to the next level. Each collected loot increases the score, while being hit by enemies decreases the score and the player's health. Different enemies decrease health and score by varying amounts, adding to the game's challenge.
